About ODPI

The Otsuka Digital Pioneer Initiative (ODPI) was created to accelerate Otsuka's digital health capabilities through active and strategic engagement with early-stage companies focused on improving mental health and well-being.

The OPDI will focus investment in these selected areas: Data & Advanced Analytics (AI); Patient Experience and Support Platforms; Digital Biomarkers, Connected Health; Remote Patient Monitoring and Digital Interventions.
