Investing in Novel Treatments for Better Health

The McQuade Center for Strategic Research and Development, LLC (MSRD) was established in 2019 by the Otsuka US Pharmaceutical Business. MSRD is named for Dr. Robert McQuade, Chief Strategic Officer for Otsuka in the US and President of MSRD.

MSRD helps advance studies in:

CENTRAL NERVOUS SYSTEM

RENAL

AREAS OF UNDERSERVED

MEDICAL NEEDS

"Otsuka is dedicated to investing in innovative and creative products that contribute to the lives of people everywhere. MSRD was created as an extension of this commitment to support and identify early stage opportunities that can change the landscape of how we treat mental illness and renal disorders in the future."

Dr. Robert McQuade

Chief Strategic Officer, Otsuka US
President of MSRD

Our Mission

The purpose of MSRD is to search for, identify and fund innovative early-stage research/development programs that have the potential to build the future portfolio of Otsuka products.

Our Approach

DISCOVERY

Search for early stage, creative assets and programs

DIALOGUE

Establish open communication and agile collaboration

DELIVERY

Invest in novel treatments that address unmet medical need
